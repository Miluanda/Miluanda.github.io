RE/Portal

This study investigates social and spatial formation (F formation) of human-teleprescence robot interaction and teleprescence robot - teleprescence robot interaction while naviaging different ways of working, collaborating and completing tasks in a remote context with second and third person point of view.

Keywords: Mobile remote presence systems(MRPs), human computer interaction, teleprescenece robot, embodied interactions, spatial awareness

Role and results

  • Designed electronic circuits (UWBs and remote motor control)
  • Designed and fabricated 3D models
  • Testing and moderating user study
  • Co-editing CSCW paper 2023
