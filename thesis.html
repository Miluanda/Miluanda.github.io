Seen for unforseeable

2022

Seen for unforeseeable is an interactive installation, a machine that transcribes cultural yeast's biological data (metabolic cycle and yeast strains) into audio and data visualization. This project explores interfaces for domesticated yeast and other microbes to be seen and honors the magic of women's work and their practices of using the kitchen as a bio lab.

Keywords: Domestication of yeasts, intimacy and mindfulness when interacting with microbes, microbes wisdom, individualism and collectivism, magic of women's work, duality and intersectionality
